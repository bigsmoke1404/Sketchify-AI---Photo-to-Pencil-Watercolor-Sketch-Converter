About Sketchify AI

Learn how image processing algorithms transform your photos into digital art.

What is Image Processing?

Image processing involves applying mathematical transformations to pixel data to produce an enhanced or artistically modified output. Sketchify AI uses Python's Pillow library along with NumPy array operations to simulate traditional artistic media in milliseconds.

How Pencil Sketch Works

Uses the classic dodge-blend technique:

  1. Convert image to grayscale
  2. Invert the grayscale image
  3. Apply Gaussian blur (radius = intensity slider)
  4. Dodge-blend: sketch = gray ÷ (1 − blur/255)
  5. Blend with original grayscale by edge-strength slider

How Watercolor Works

Chains multiple Pillow operations:

  1. Apply N passes of MedianFilter(size=5) for edge-preserving smoothing
  2. Boost colour saturation ×1.7 for watercolor vibrancy
  3. SMOOTH_MORE to soften harsh edges
  4. Slight contrast enhancement ×1.15
